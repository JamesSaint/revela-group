Executive Summary

Mandate

Replace volume incentives with outcome contracts. Price services on verified value. Give people readable rights and real redress.

Model

Membership, clinical services, employer programs, software licensing, and creator media. No hidden spread. No data capture without purpose.

Seed Ask

£8.5m to stand up Revela Health, publish outcomes, and prepare scale into Education, Finance, Governance, and Media.

Phase One is Health. We start where the old paradigm hurts people most. Preventative care, longitudinal recovery, clear prices, and data sovereignty. Evidence in the open.

Thesis and Mission

Thesis

When incentives, information, and accountability align, outcomes improve. We align them with open interfaces, consent that means something, and audits that are not optional.

Mission

Design and scale ethical operating models for Health, Education, Finance, Governance, and Media. Start in the United Kingdom. Prove the standard. Replicate where it helps people most.

The Problem

  • Misaligned incentives. Throughput is paid. Results are optional.
  • Opacity. Contracts and data are either unreadable or gated.
  • Compliance over care. The human is an afterthought.
  • Innovation stalls behind legacy rails and policy friction.
  • Compounding social cost across health, money, learning, civic life, and media trust.

Operating Model and Why It Wins

Design Principles
  • Privacy by design with revocation that works.
  • Explainable intelligence with a human accountable for decisions.
  • Open interfaces so people can move their data and providers can be replaced.
  • Outcome pricing and readable service charters.
Defensibility
  • Trust and transparency as the moat. Public dashboards and contracts in plain language.
  • Cross pillar reuse. One consent vault and audit trail across services reduces cost and increases speed.
  • Integrity brand. People have been waiting for this category.

Pillar Architecture

Revela Health

Preventative, outcomes led care for chronic and neurological conditions. Tele clinic network, diagnostics, rehab, consent vault, explainable triage, outcome dashboards. Transparent pricing and access programs through our non profit arm.

Revela Education

Modular, lifelong learning tied to capability and proof. Health literacy, digital rights, civic skill, and regenerative enterprise first. Mentors and practice labs with verifiable progress.

Revela Finance

Plain fee services and ethical products. Fiduciary duty in writing. Tools for saving, protection, and fair capital that serves wellbeing outcomes.

Revela Governance

Practical participation kits, open policy tooling, and independent audit trails for AI and data in the public interest.

Revela Media

Evidence anchored explainers and investigations with citation chains. Tools that help people judge information quality without manipulation.

Technology and AI

Core Stack
  • Consent and data vault with fine grained purpose, time boxed access, and true revocation.
  • Explainable models with readable rationales and model cards.
  • Open APIs and cryptographic audit trails.
  • Security baseline with zero trust, least privilege, encryption in transit and at rest, and continuous monitoring.
Governance Interfaces

People, clinicians, and councils can inspect model behavior, trigger audits, and require remediation. Changes are logged and published.

Critical Role Summaries
  • Security Engineer — threat models, code reviews, monitoring, drills. Deliver playbooks, triage runbooks, vendor hardening. KPIs include find to fix time and drill performance. Requirement is five or more years in regulated data.
  • Head of Content — editorial integrity across pillars. Deliver calendars, investigations plan, style guide. KPIs include organic growth and zero retractions. Requirement is seven or more years with investigations or standards.
  • AI and ML Engineer — safe recommendations, explainability, bias evaluation. Deliver model cards and evaluation suites. KPIs include lift vs baseline and fairness metrics. Requirement is production ML with human centered systems.
