1. Executive Summary

Revela Group is a public benefit enterprise headquartered in the United Kingdom with a clear mandate: build truthful, transparent, and human-centered systems where existing institutions have drifted from their purpose. We operate across five essential domains of life: Health, Education, Finance, Governance, and Media. Each domain is a business line with its own leadership, revenue engine, and measurable impact. Together they form a coherent architecture that restores trust and capability at scale.

Phase One launches Revela Health as the flagship. We are designing a preventative, data-sovereign, clinically rigorous healthcare ecosystem that aligns incentives to outcomes, not throughput. This includes ethical data practices, longitudinal care pathways, decentralised diagnostics, and transparent pricing. Our clinical model pairs evidence-based medicine with a whole-human approach that respects individual agency.

Revela Group is structured as a hybrid entity: a for-profit public benefit corporation with a non-profit arm for research, standards, and public interest programs. This allows commercial scale while protecting mission integrity. Our plan details the operating model, market entry, technology, governance, hiring, and five-year financials. We are raising an initial seed round to stand up the Health pillar, deliver proof points, and ready the Group platform for additional pillars.

2. Vision and Mission

Vision

A world where the systems that shape daily life are worthy of human dignity. Truth is knowable, care is accountable, learning is lifelong, capital is fair, governance is participatory, and media amplifies reality rather than distorting it.

Mission

Design, build, and scale ethical operating models across Health, Education, Finance, Governance, and Media. Begin with Health, prove integrity and outcomes, then replicate principles and platforms across the remaining pillars.

We measure success through real outcomes for people, transparency of practice, and resilience of the systems we create.

3. The Problem

  • Misaligned incentives. Volume is rewarded over outcomes. Financial structures often separate decision making from accountability.
  • Opaque information. Data and narratives are fragmented or gated. Trust erodes when people cannot see how decisions are made.
  • Human needs sidelined. Institutions optimise for compliance and cost containment. People become case numbers rather than lives in context.
  • Slow innovation. Legacy infrastructure and policy friction stall necessary change. The status quo persists even when evidence is clear.
  • Social cost. Declining health outcomes, inequity in education, predatory finance, disengaged governance, and polarised media generate compounding harm.

4. Our Solution and Model

Revela Group aligns design, incentives, and accountability. We build operating models that place people at the center and make the system transparent by default. Each pillar shares common infrastructure: ethical data standards, explainable AI, open interfaces, and clear service charters. Commercial models reward outcomes and verified value creation.

  • Ethical architecture. Privacy-by-design, data minimisation, verifiable consent, and independent oversight.
  • Explainable intelligence. Models that clinicians and citizens can interrogate. Human responsible, machine assisted.
  • Open contracts. Clear commitments to quality, price, and redress. Agreements that regular people can read.
  • Aligned incentives. Payment and participation structures that reward outcomes, not activity alone.
  • Modular platforms. Shared capabilities across pillars to reduce duplication and accelerate reuse.

5. Pillar Architecture

Revela Health

Purpose. Deliver preventative, data-sovereign, outcomes-led care. Combine clinical excellence with lived-experience design. Start in the UK, extend globally through partnerships.

Core capabilities. Longitudinal care plans, tele-clinic network, diagnostic partnerships, therapy and rehabilitation programs, ethical data vaults, explainable triage and pathway tools, transparent pricing, and outcome dashboards.

Products and services. Subscription care plans, clinics, remote monitoring, digital therapeutics, specialist programs for chronic and neurological conditions, employer health packages, and licensed software for partner providers.

Revela Education

Lifelong, modular learning with real assessments of capability. AI-supported mentors, practice labs, and verifiable micro-credentials. Focus on health literacy, digital rights, civic skill, and regenerative enterprise.

Revela Finance

Transparent, ethical financial products and services. Fair capital flows, savings and protection tools, and financing aligned with wellbeing outcomes. Clear risk, plain language, and independent fiduciary duties.

Revela Governance

Participatory decision frameworks for organisations and communities. Open policy tooling, independent audit trails, and citizen oversight interfaces. Practical guardrails for AI and data in the public interest.

Revela Media

Evidence-anchored storytelling and public service journalism. Narrative integrity protocols, transparent sourcing, and tools that help citizens evaluate information quality without manipulation.

7. Market Opportunity

Revela Group operates where human need and institutional fatigue intersect with mature digital capability. Health spends continue to rise while outcomes lag. Employers seek resilient teams. Individuals want clarity and real help. Similar tailwinds exist across Education, Finance, Governance, and Media. Starting in the UK creates a pragmatic sandbox with global relevance. Platform components scale internationally with local partners and regulatory alignment.

8. Rollout and Go-to-Market (Phase One: Revela Health)

Phase One Objectives

  • Stand up a tele-clinic and specialist network focused on chronic and neurological conditions.
  • Deploy the consent vault, outcome dashboards, and explainable triage tools.
  • Launch three signature programs: Neurological Care, Respiratory Resilience, and Pain Pathways.
  • Sign ten employer pilots and two insurer partnerships.

Go-to-Market Motions

  • Clinical credibility first. Senior clinicians and published protocols. Transparent case series.
  • Employer channels. Absence reduction, productivity gains, duty of care compliance.
  • Patient advocacy and communities. Lived-experience co-design and service councils.
  • Ethical brand voice. Clear promises, honest limits, and visible listening loops.

9. Technology and AI Framework

  • Consent and Data Vault. Individuals control who sees what, for how long, and for what purpose. Revocation is real.
  • Explainable Models. Transparent triage and pathway guidance with human-readable rationales. Clinical override always available.
  • Interoperability. Open APIs, standards alignment, and cryptographic audit trails across parties.
  • Security. Zero-trust principles, least privilege, encryption at rest and in transit, continuous monitoring.
  • Governance Interfaces. Citizen panels and clinician oversight can review and test behaviors of systems.

10. Governance and Ownership

Revela Group operates as a for-profit public benefit corporation with a non-profit arm dedicated to research, public standards, and access programs. The Group Charter encodes duties to people and outcomes. Executive compensation ties to mission metrics as well as financial results. An independent Ethics Council has authority to audit, publish findings, and recommend corrective action.

Body Scope Powers
Board of Directors Strategy, risk, capital allocation, CEO oversight. Approve budgets, appoint leadership, ratify charter changes.
Ethics Council Integrity, data, model behavior, service charters. Independent audits, public reporting, binding remediation recommendations.
Domain Councils Pillar-specific quality and outcomes oversight. Set service standards, review results, approve updates.
Non-Profit Arm Research, scholarships, access, and public standards. Grantmaking and program delivery aligned to charter.
